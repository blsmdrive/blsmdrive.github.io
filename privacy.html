Privacy Policy

At Dayflux, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our breathing and mindfulness app.

Information We Collect

Health and Fitness Data

With your permission, Dayflux may collect and store:

  • Breathing session data (duration, frequency, types of exercises)
  • Wellness metrics and progress tracking information
  • HealthKit integration data (when you choose to sync with Apple Health)

Usage Information

We may collect information about how you use our app, including:

  • App features accessed and frequency of use
  • Session preferences and settings
  • App performance and crash data to improve functionality

Subscription Information

For premium features, we collect:

  • Subscription status and purchase history
  • Device identifiers for subscription management
  • User identifiers for account continuity

How We Use Your Information

App Functionality

  • Provide personalized breathing exercises and recommendations
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Sync data with Apple HealthKit (when authorized)
  • Manage your subscription and premium features
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Fix bugs and optimize app performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party services (like RevenueCat for subscription management) that help us operate our app
  • HealthKit: Health data is stored locally on your device and in your personal HealthKit data, which is controlled by you
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Health data is stored securely using Apple's HealthKit framework
  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • We regularly review and update our security practices
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only

Your Rights and Choices

HealthKit Data

  • You control all HealthKit permissions and can revoke access at any time
  • Health data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete health data through the Apple Health app

App Data

  • You can delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • You can opt out of analytics and crash reporting in app settings
  • Subscription data is managed through your Apple ID

Third-Party Services

Dayflux integrates with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and analytics
  • Apple HealthKit: For health data storage and synchronization
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and payment processing

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Dayflux is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

If you are using Dayflux outside of the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located and our service providers operate.
